The emergence of the term artificial intelligence dates back to the fifties of the twentieth century, and specifically in the year 1950 AD when the scientist Alan Turing introduced what is known as the Turing Test, which is concerned with assessing the intelligence of a computer and classifying it as smart in the case of its ability On simulating the human mind
